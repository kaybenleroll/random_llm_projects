# SILO RPG PLAYER REFERENCE

Player-safe quick sheet for character creation, table reminders, and everyday assumptions.

## The Three Absolute Truths

1. **The Outside is Death:** The world above is toxic and dead.
2. **The Pact is Law:** The Pact governs every part of life.
3. **The Cleaning is Our Burden:** The condemned clean the sensors before dying outside.

## The Three Sectors

| Sector | Levels | Vibe | People | Main Danger |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Up Top** | 1-48 | Bright, clean, sterile | Mayor, Sheriff, Judicial, IT | Surveillance |
| **The Mids** | 49-96 | Humid, crowded, earthy | Workers, farmers, Medical, Supply | Starvation and disease |
| **Down Deep** | 97-144 | Hot, loud, oily | Mechanics, miners, drillers | Industrial death |

## How the Silo Feels

### What a Level Usually Contains

* **Stair landing:** traffic, rest, gossip, visibility
* **Inner corridors:** pipes, ducts, utility access
* **Lived spaces:** homes, offices, workshops, farms, clinics, tanks
* **Outer wall:** thick concrete, patched wiring, storage, pressure of the earth

### Distance Between Levels

About **50 stairs** sit between official levels.

* **1-3 levels:** local movement
* **5-10 levels:** a real trip
* **10+ levels:** notable effort
* **20+ levels:** serious travel

### Split Levels and Dead Spaces

* **Split levels:** one official level with very different functional halves
* **Half-levels / service spaces:** unofficial in-between rooms and utility bands
* **Structural seams:** levels that creak, flex, and feel heavily patched
* **Dead spaces:** vents, flooded corners, abandoned rooms, and sealed areas off the public map

## Core Roles

* **Porter:** Stairs runner, courier, rumor mill.
* **Mechanic:** Keeps machines, scrubbers, and generators alive.
* **Supply Clerk:** Controls forms, ledgers, shortages, and favors.
* **Hydroponics Farmer:** Grows food and understands plants, toxins, and yield.
* **IT Tech / Medical Assistant:** Has access to rare knowledge, terminals, or medicine.

## What Every Citizen Knows

* A trip of more than ten levels is serious travel.
* Stamped paper usually beats spoken truth.
* Every level has a fixer, a gossip, and someone suspected of informing.
* Strange light pulses mean something bad or official is happening.
* Porters know routes, Mechanical knows faults, Supply knows lies, Medical knows desperation, and IT knows too much.
* Children are given ancestor names at age five in a Gifting ceremony.

## What a Normal Day Feels Like

* Queue for water.
* Work your shift.
* Trade small favors all day.
* Hear rumors before mid-cycle.
* Measure distance in sore legs.
* Fall asleep wondering who on your level can really be trusted.

## Session Zero Questions

1. Which person on your level depends on your wages to survive?
2. Which department do you distrust on principle?
3. What minor law did you break as a child and get away with?
4. Who taught you how to survive the stairs, queues, or inspections?
5. What rumor about the Silo do you secretly hope is true?
6. What debt, favor, or shame keeps you from walking away from trouble?
